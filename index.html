About Me

Chelsey is a bi-lingual full stack developer who is passionate about coding. She is originally from Tennessee, but currently lives in Minnesota. Chelsey is a recent graduate of the University of Minnesota’s Full Stack Coding Bootcamp. She thrives with leaders and working environments that foster creativity and individuality. Chelsey has also been commended for her excellent customer service skills, and quick problem-solving.

She first began coding when she entered in google’s annual ‘girls who code’ competition, which allows teams of girls from around the world to create an app that can improve their community. After the contest piqued her interest in computer programming, she began online classes through Code Academy and Khan Academy. Looking for a more supportive, and female led environment, she found the Minneapolis chapter of Girl Develop It. Through the classes offred by Girl Develop It, Chelsey began to learn the basics of front-end technologies such as HTML5 + CSS3, Javascript, jQuery, and Boostrap. Girl Develop It gave Chelsey the confidence to apply for a coding bootcamp, which offers a rigorous curriculum. She is still an avid member of Girl Develop It Minneapolis, where she is currently a chapter leader, and an avid attendant of GDI’s weekly Tuesday meetup, Toast & Tech.The technologies Chelsey is comfortable using are front-end technologies HTML5,CSS3, JavaScript, and other design frameworks such as Bootstrap. As well as back-end technologies such as Node.js, Express.js, Firebase, and mySQL.
